What is Polymaze?

POLYMAZE is a maze-solving competition hosted by the Vision & Innovation Club. Designed robots rely on their programming and sensors to independently navigate the maze, showcasing impressive robotic abilities.

What's new in POLYMAZE ?

We haven't made a ton of changes to the fundamental structure, the rules, robot specifications, and registration process stay the same. However, here's the exciting part: POLYMAZE will be in two days this year! The first day will consist of the qualification rounds, while the second day will feature the semi finals and the finals.The dates are going to be announced on our site and all over our social media platforms.
